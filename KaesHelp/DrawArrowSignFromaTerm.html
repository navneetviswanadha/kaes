Draw Arrow, "=" Sign From a Term

Click on the kin term in the window on the left that will be the kin term from which the arrow or "=" sign originates. The selected term will be marked with a dashed rectangle.

Next click on the kin term in the window on the left that will be the kin term to which the arrow or "=" sign terminates.

When done with the selected arrow or equal sign, or to cancel the arrow selected, press the right mouse button. This will reactivate the menu at the bottom of the screen.
