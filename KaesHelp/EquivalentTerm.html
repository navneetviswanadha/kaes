Equivalent Term

The term equivalent to another kin term is enclosed in quotation makes and listed in the Workspace window. The effect of unsplitting a kin term that has previously been split into a term and its equivalent term is to make the term and its equivalent term into distinct kin terms. After selecting the equivalent term that is to be unsplit, you will be asked to provide a new name for the equivalent term. You may use any name other than the name for a kin term already included in the kin term map.
