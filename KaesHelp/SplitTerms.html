Split Terms

Click on the term to be split. You will be asked to verify that you do want to split the kin term. The new term is drawn near the term that is being split and may be treated in the same manner as any other kin term. The added term will not appear in the listing in the right window until the current level of the program is exited and the Construct option is re-selected. Once a term has been split it cannot be split again.
