Erase Entries

Click on the term, arrow or affine connector to be erased. When erasing a term, all arrows originating or terminating at that term will also be erased. When a term is selected you will be asked to verify that you do want to erase the kin term. The erased term is then removed from the map and reactivated in the kin term list. It is not deleted from the kin term list.

Confirmation will not be solicited when individual arrows or the affine connector ("=") between a pair of terms is to be erased.
