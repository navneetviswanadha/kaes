Classificatory Eqtns.

Some terminologies, such as the Trobriand Terminology, have kin terms that can be glossed "Older Brother," "Older Sister," etc. where these kin terms are also generating elements. In such cases, the genealogy algorithm introduces corresponding primary kin types; e.g., b+ is the kin type corresponding to "Older Brother." When there are classificatory kin term equations such as "Older Brother" of Father = Father, it may be useful to simplify the list of kin type products by introducing a parallel equation at the level of kin types; e.g., fb+ = f.
