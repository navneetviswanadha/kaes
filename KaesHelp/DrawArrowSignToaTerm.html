Draw Arrow, "=" Sign To a Term

Click on the kin term in the window on the left that will be the kin term to which the arrow or "=" sign terminates. For multiple arrows originating from the same kin term but ending at different kin terms, keep selecting the additional terminating kin terms.

Press the right mouse button when there are no more terminating terms to be selected in conjunction with the active originating term, or to cancel the kin term selected as the originating kin term.
