Iso. Comparison

The algebraic structure and the kin term map are found to be isomorphic based upon a comparison of the two structures. In some cases one structure is more extensive than the other in terms of number of steps away from the focal term displayed in the structure. In such a case the comparison may be ambiguous. The KAES program will inform the user if the isomorphic comparison has ambiguities. If there are ambiguities, they will be displayed in the next screen.
