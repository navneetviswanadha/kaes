Kin Term Product

The definition of a kin term product does not depend upon the genealogical relationship of ego to alter1, alter1 to alter2, or ego to alter2. Instead, it is based on the conceptual linkages among kin terms that allows individuals to know the proper kin terms from the way the kin terms are used by one person for another person without necessarily referring first to a genealogical space. The kinship terminology is viewed as a conceptual structure with its own logic and the goal of the analysis is to make that logic observable through constructing an algebraic structure isomorphic to the kin term structure.
